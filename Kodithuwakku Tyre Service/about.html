About us

Welcome to Kodithuwakku Tyre Service, a family-owned and operated tire haven with a legacy of excellence spanning over 30 years. Our founder and owner, Mr. Chandrasiri Kodithuwakku, has been a vehicle enthusiast since a young age, and that passion has grown into a thriving business.

At Kodithuwakku Tyre Service, we recognize that your vehicle's tires are a crucial component for performance, safety, and comfort. Our tire inventory is thoughtfully curated, encompassing a wide array of options from trusted brands, ensuring you find the perfect fit for your car, truck, or SUV, all tailored to suit your budget.

Our team of highly skilled and certified technicians stands ready to maintain the reliability and safety of your vehicle. From professional tire installations and repairs to precise wheel balancing and alignment services, we're equipped to ensure your tires are in optimal condition.

We take our environmental responsibilities to heart by adhering to eco-friendly tire disposal practices, reflecting our commitment to both our local community and the planet. But our commitment extends beyond tires; it's about people. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is here to assist you, offering expert advice, addressing your inquiries, and providing personalized recommendations to ensure you make the right tire choices for your specific driving needs.

At Kodithuwakku Tyre Service, your satisfaction is our priority, and your safety is our mission. We look forward to welcoming you to our shop, where you can experience the difference that quality, expertise, and exceptional customer service can make in your driving life. Thank you for choosing Kodithuwakku Tyre Service as your trusted tire destination.
